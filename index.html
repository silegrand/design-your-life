Before the last few questions, a plain and honest word about what Assistiv is.

Assistiv can help you and those you care about feel more reassured about day-to-day safety at home. It can support you to stay connected with people who matter. It can help you keep track of your own health and wellbeing in a way you control. It can offer gentle support for memory, activity and routines if you would like that.

Assistiv cannot replace human care. It cannot make decisions about your care without your knowledge. It cannot share information with anyone you have not chosen.
